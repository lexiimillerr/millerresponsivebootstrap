Black Sheep Coffee

Located in downtown Harrisonburg, Black Sheep Coffee first opened its doors after owner and founder Chance Ebersold moved to Harrisonburg from Maine. The coffee shop features a variety of items such as coffees, teas, pastries, and breakfast foods.

Greenberry's Coffee

After its first location in Charrlotsville, VA proved to be a success, entrapenuers Sean and Roxanne Simmons decided to bring their business to Harrisonburg. Over twnety-seven years later, Greenberry's continues to thrive.

Broad Porch Coffee

Native to Luray, VA, this mostly wholesale coffee company specializes in keeping its customers stocked up. Here you can enjoy a great cup of coffee and get a glimpse of the roastery process right in front of you!

Visit Website

Chestnut Ridge Coffee

Harrisonburg's newest coffee shop, Chestnut Ridge Coffee, has only been around for five years but has since taken off. Connected to Magpie's Diner, this coffee shop is the perfect place to hangout while enduring that diner line!

Visit Website

Merge Coffee Co.

As another fairly young coffee shop, Merge Coffee Co. prides itself in serving and supporting the Harrisonburg community. The idea of Merge came after two family friends decided to combine their love for coffee and start their own business.
